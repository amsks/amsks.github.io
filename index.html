§ About Me

  • I am a Master's student in the EIT Digital Master's course in Autonomous systems. I am interested in exploring how learning systems can learn to abstract structure out of tasks and learn to reason about themselves. What excites me the most is the extent to which we can learn about our intelligence through the endeavor of creating one. I have worked on problems at the intersection of Robotics, Reinforcement Learning, and Meta-Learning in single-agent and Multi-Agent settings, and I have also dabbled with Evolutionary Algorithms and Optimization Literature. I am currently working on ad-hoc cooperation in the game of Hanabi
